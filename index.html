Who we are?

In 2008, Binod Verma & Co. was established by CA. Binod Verma, a trailblazer in the Chartered Accountancy industry in India. For more than two decades, our firm has remained committed to delivering exceptional professional services to clients while adhering to the profession's code of ethics and conduct. These fundamental values have been the cornerstone of our growth and expansion as we strive to exceed our clients' expectations.

At BinodVerma & Company, we prioritize client satisfaction by delivering high-quality services with efficient communication and on-time delivery. Our clients trust us for our reliable and trustworthy service, and we take great pride in maintaining our reputation.

Our services include audit and assurance, business taxation, finance, accounting, GST, and much more. Our team of experienced professionals is well-equipped to handle any accounting challenge your business may face, and we always ensure that our clients' needs are met with the utmost care and attention.
